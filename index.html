Backtrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

Overview

Backtrack ("we", "our", or "the app") is a checklist application designed to help you build habits and manage tasks. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Backtrack does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Data Storage

Your templates, tasks, and settings are stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • In iCloud (if you have iCloud enabled) for syncing across your devices

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Your data is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures.

Third-Party Services

Backtrack uses the following Apple services:

  • iCloud - For syncing your data across devices (optional)
  • StoreKit - For processing in-app purchases
  • Push Notifications - For due date reminders (optional)

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Backtrack does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
